Tourist Visa & Top Attractions

Explore the world: tourist visa essentials and must-see destinations

Published on March 12, 2026

What is a Tourist Visa?

A tourist visa allows you to visit a country for leisure, sightseeing, and experiencing its culture. It is typically valid for a short period and does not permit employment or study.

Popular Tourist Visa Destinations

  • France: Explore Paris, the Eiffel Tower, and the French Riviera
  • Japan: Visit Tokyo, Kyoto, cherry blossoms, and Mount Fuji
  • Italy: Discover Rome, Venice, Florence, and the Amalfi Coast
  • USA: See New York, Grand Canyon, and theme parks
  • Australia: Experience Sydney, the Great Barrier Reef, and Gold Coast
  • Thailand: Enjoy Bangkok, Phuket, and ancient temples

Tourist Visa Requirements

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Completed visa application form
  • Proof of accommodation and travel itinerary
  • Financial proof to cover expenses
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Visa fee payment

Application Process

Step 1: Choose your destination and check visa requirements

Step 2: Fill out the visa application form

Step 3: Gather required documents (passport, proof of funds, itinerary)

Step 4: Submit your application online or at the embassy

Step 5: Pay the visa fee

Step 6: Attend an interview if required

Step 7: Receive your visa and start your adventure!

Top Attractions to Visit

  • France: Louvre Museum, Disneyland Paris, French Alps
  • Japan: Shibuya Crossing, Fushimi Inari Shrine, Osaka Castle
  • Italy: Colosseum, Leaning Tower of Pisa, Cinque Terre
  • USA: Statue of Liberty, Yellowstone National Park, Las Vegas Strip
  • Australia: Sydney Opera House, Uluru, Bondi Beach
  • Thailand: Phi Phi Islands, Grand Palace, Floating Markets

Tips for a Great Trip

  • Apply for your visa well in advance
  • Check local customs and travel advisories
  • Keep digital and physical copies of documents
  • Purchase travel insurance
  • Respect local laws and traditions
